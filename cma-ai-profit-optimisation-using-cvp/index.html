Key Terms: do not ask “Can AI calculate this?” Ask “What question is AI calculating?”

AI can collect data and draw charts quickly. The danger in management accounting is rarely the arithmetic. The danger is the hidden assumption.

Break-even is a visual story before it is a formula

Before selling the first unit, the business is already losing money equal to fixed cost. Each unit contributes only what remains after variable cost.

Contribution margin is a moving target

In an auto dealership, average CMU comes from product mix, accessories, insurance attachment, dealer subsidy and demand sensitivity. A price discount is not only a margin decision. It is a volume bet.

Showroom Dashboard

The same CMU assumption can produce very different economics across showrooms because each location has a different fixed-cost base and baseline volume.
